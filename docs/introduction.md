# 🚀 Introduction

Asgard is an **"business-in-a-box"** platform designed for global startups and crypto-native companies. It abstracts the complexity of the Stellar blockchain into a professional, self-hosted dashboard that lets businesses:

*   💸 **Run global payroll** in USDC with automatic fiat conversion via Stellar Path Payments and SEP-31 Anchor protocols.
*   📄 **Issue smart invoices** with optional milestone-based Soroban escrow contracts.
*   📦 **Track inventory** as tokenized assets issued directly on the Stellar Ledger.

No third-party payroll processor, no forex broker, no lock-in. Just a Stellar wallet, Docker, and this repo.

---

### Core Philosophy
Asgard's primary goal is to make the Stellar network accessible to business owners who are not necessarily blockchain experts. We focus on:
1. **Self-Sobriety:** You own your keys and your data.
2. **Global Reach:** Use Stellar's anchor network for instant cross-border payouts.
3. **Smart Invoicing:** Automate trust with programmable escrow contracts.
